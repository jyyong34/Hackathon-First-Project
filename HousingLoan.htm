Welcome to Signature Pharmacy, the retail pharmacy

that specializes in skin care!


Established in 2021, Signature Pharmacy has become a centre of reference

for public in skin care and minor skin ailments management. The outlet is

founded and managed by a highly qualified pharmacist Mr.Lim who was graduated

from UCSI university in year 2016. Mr. Lim worked as hospital pharmacist at

Hospital Kuala Lumpur at Department of Dermatology for 5 years and is

well-versed in knowledge for various skin ailments management. Should you

have any skin problems, you are always welcome to drop a visit at Signature

Pharmacy and our pharmacist will be there to serve you.
